Hi, my name is Johannes, and I'm currently a second year PhD Student at the William Harvey Research Institute in London. My project investigates lymphocyte dynamics during the perioperative period. I've completed my undergraduate degree in Medical Genetics at Queen Mary University of London. Besides immunology I'm interested in all things data science. This blog serves as my personal dumpsite for projects and articles.
