Hi, my name is Johannes, and I'm a postdoc at the William Harvey Research Institute in London. I've completed my PhD in immunology and undergraduate degree in Medical Genetics at Queen Mary University of London. Besides immunology I'm interested in all things data science. This blog serves as my personal archive for projects and ideas.
